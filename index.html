N-body simulation

This simulation shows the interaction of particles orbiting an oppositely charged super-massive particle, where one of the orbiting particles has twice the charge of each of the other orbiting particles. You may view an X3D animation in your web browser here.

A larger simulation of 110,000 particles shows the interactions of electron beamlets surrounded by a halo. The spokes that appear in a cross section of the particle distribution are similar to those that appeared in experiments done using rubidium ions. Here is a movie showing the propagation of the particle densities.
